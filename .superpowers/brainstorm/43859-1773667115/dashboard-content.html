Dashboard Home Content

What should fill the main content area on the desktop dashboard?

1

Stats Overview Row

Top-level numbers: active pools, total monthly contributions, upcoming payouts, total members across all pools. Shown as stat cards in a row.

2

Pools Grid/List

All your pools displayed as cards in a grid (not a single-column list). Each card shows pool name, status, progress, amount, and member count.

3

Current Cycle / Action Items

A panel showing what needs your attention: pending payments, pools waiting for your contribution, upcoming payout dates. Actionable items front and center.

4

Recent Notifications / Activity Feed

A sidebar panel showing latest activity: new payments submitted, members joined, announcements. Quick glanceable feed.

5

Upcoming Schedule

A calendar-like or timeline view of upcoming payout dates across all pools. Helps plan finances at a glance.

6

Pending Invitations

A small section showing any pool invitations waiting for your response, with accept/decline actions.

Select all that you want on the dashboard. Click multiple items.
